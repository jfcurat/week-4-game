Crystals Collector!

You'll be given a random number at start of game.

There are 4 crystals. Clicking a crystal will add a specific amount of points to your score.

You win by matching your score to the random target score. You lose by going over the target score.

The value of each crystal will be hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game restarts, the values of the crystals will change, and you'll get a new target score.
